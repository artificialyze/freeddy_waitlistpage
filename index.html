You add a client to Freeddy. You get two links — one for you, one for them. You send theirs. They click it. They're inside their own branded Room, addressed by name, with a welcome message already waiting.

No account creation. No password reset. No "I didn't get the email." No tutorial.

The Magic Link

Your client gets a single URL that is theirs forever. They click it from any device, any browser, any country. They are immediately inside the Project Room — no friction, no gatekeeping. This is the end of the abandoned portal.

The Project Room

Everything for the project lives in one place. Chat that feels like WhatsApp. Contracts they can sign in 30 seconds. Invoices with a Pay Now button. Files organised by type. Milestones they can actually see. The Room replaces five tools and the chaos between them.

Contracts Without The Awkward Email

Use a template or upload your own. Send it as a card in the Room. Your client reads it, signs it with a typed or drawn signature, and you both get a timestamped PDF. No DocuSign account required. No PDF buried in a Downloads folder. Done in under two minutes.

Invoices That Actually Get Paid

Create an invoice, send it into the Room as a card. Your client sees the total and a Pay Now button. In India, they pay via UPI, Net Banking, or card through Razorpay. Internationally, Stripe handles it. You get a real-time notification. The invoice card updates to Paid. No chasing. No awkward "just following up."

The Revision Counter

Set your revision limit when you create the Room. Your client can see "Revisions Used: 1 of 3" at all times. When they hit the limit, Freeddy sends them a graceful notification automatically. Scope creep is handled by the system, not by an uncomfortable conversation.

The Project Archive

When a project closes, Freeddy generates a complete archive — every file, every signed contract, every paid invoice, the full chat transcript — and sends it to both of you as a downloadable ZIP. Your client has never been treated this professionally by a freelancer. They will tell someone.

Fair questions.
Is the product actually built?

Yes. The CRM, the Magic Links, and the Project Room chat are live and working today. Contracts, invoicing, and the full feature suite are shipping over the next six weeks. Founding members get access the moment each feature goes live — before anyone else.

Why is it only $9/month?

Because you're early. You're taking a chance on something that isn't finished yet. The lifetime price is how we say thank you for that. Once we launch publicly the price goes to $19/month and the lifetime deal closes permanently.

What makes this different from Dubsado or HoneyBook?

Those tools are built for the freelancer's side of the relationship. Freeddy is built for both sides. Specifically: your client never needs to create an account or remember a password. They click one link and they're in. That single difference changes the entire experience — for you and for them.

What if I already have a system that works?

Then you probably don't need Freeddy. This is for freelancers whose clients keep drifting back to WhatsApp despite having a portal, who are duct-taping five tools together, and who have never had a client relationship that felt truly organised. If that's not you, we genuinely respect that.

Is my client's data secure?

Yes. Every Space is protected by Row Level Security at the database layer — not just the application. A client can only ever access their specific Space. No client sees another client's data. Ever.
